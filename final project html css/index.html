About Me

My name is Petya Mihaylova and I am a Cloud Solutions Architect with agile and project-management skills, as well as knowledge and multiple certifications in Cloud Security, DevOps and Networking, both in Azure, AWS and Google. I have both a technical (over 5 years of experience) and customer-facing background (over 8 years of experience). I strive to constantly learn and develop, as well as deliver excellence in my day-to-day activities at work and beyond. I believe that professional success is something to be achieved both on an individual level and within the team, while helping one another be the best versions of ourselves. In my free time, I enjoy spending time with my family, watching martial arts movies and working out.
